How to generate number frequency by category?

Go to Frequency tab, upon loading of frequency results, just tap the more options from upper right corner of the screen then select your desire settings.

How to generate lucky picks numbers?

Go to Lucky Picks' tab then the app automatically generates a lucky picks numbers for every game modes, you can refresh the lucky picks numbers by tapping the Refresh icon from the upper right corner of the screen.

How to receive notification on a specific game modes?

Go to Notification's Tab and select your desire game modes, you would know that it's turned on when the selected game toggle becomes colored green.
